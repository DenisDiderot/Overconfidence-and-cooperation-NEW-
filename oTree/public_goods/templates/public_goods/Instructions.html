Instructions

This second part of the experiment consists of 12 rounds. In each of them you are going to make a single decision. One of the rounds will be randomly chosen for the payment of the second part. In each one you will be randomly paired with one other participant.

In each round both you and your group partner will be endowed with 100 tokens. You can choose either to allocate them in a group project or to keep them for yourself (every combination of integer numbers is possible). If the current round is selected, each playerâ€™s payoff will be the sum of the amount of money kept for himself/herself plus the earnings from the group project. The earnings from the group project are calculated as follows: both players invest part of their money in the project, their sum is multiplied by a factor alpha and then the resulting amount is returned to both players. Therefore each player will receive the same amount of money from the group project.

Alpha will depend on how well you or your partner performed in part 1. More specifically it will simply be the number of players who are strictly worse than you or your partner, divided by ten (the number of participants), as shown in the following table.

Number of players who are strictly worse than you Alpha
0 0
1 0.1
2 0.2
... ...
5 0.5
6 0.6
... ...
9 0.9

The payoff of this part will therefore be

Payoff from part 2 = 100 - your contribution + alpha*(your contribution + your partner's contribution)

Remember that before the Contribution page is shown, you will be informed about the player whose performance will be relevant for alpha. If your partner's performance determines alpha, then you will also be informed about alpha. However, if it is based upon your performance, you will not receive any further information.

At the end of the game only one of the rounds is going to be selected and you will be explicitly told which one, with the payoff in detail. Immediately afterwards you will have to fill in a quick survey and then you will be paid by the experimenters. Please remain in your cubicle until your number is called.
