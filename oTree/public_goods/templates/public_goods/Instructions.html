Instructions

This second part of the experiment consists of 12 rounds. In each of them you are going to make a single decision. One of these will be randomly chosen for the payment of the second part. In each round you will be randomly paired with one other participant.

In each round both you and your group partner will be endowed with 100 tokens. You can choose either to allocate them in a group project or to keep it for yourself (every combination of integer numbers is possible). If the current round is selected, each playerâ€™s payoff at the end of the round will be the sum of the amount of money kept for himself plus half of the group project. This works as follows: both players invest part of their money in it, their sum is multiplied by a factor alpha and then the resulting amount is returned to both players. Therefore each player will receive the same amount of money from the group project. Alpha will be the decile in which either you or your opponent (depending on the information you will receive) were in the previous task. Suppose it is determined by your performance and you ended up in the 5th decile, then alpha will be 0.5. In case it is determined by your opponent's performance you will be informed about its value, for instance 7th decile, and the alpha will consequently be 0.7. The payoff of this part will therefore be

Payoff from task 2i = 100 - your contribution + alpha*(your contribution + your partner's contribution)

where alpha is either the decile of player i or of player j from task 1 and c the contribution of the players. Remember that before the Contribution page is shown, you will be informed about the player upon whom the alpha will be calculated.

At the end of the game only one of the rounds is going to be selected and you will be explicitly told which one, with the payoff in detail. Immediately afterwards you will have to fill in a quick survey and then you will be paid by the experimenters. Please remain in your cubicle until your number is called.
